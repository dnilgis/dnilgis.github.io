What Is Basis?
Basis is the difference between your local cash price and the nearest CME futures contract. A negative basis (e.g. −30¢) means local cash is 30 cents below futures.
Why Basis Matters
Stronger (less negative) basis means more local demand. Track basis trends to decide when to sell and whether to use basis contracts.
Compare Elevators
Even a 5¢ difference in basis at elevators 20 miles apart can be significant on a big crop. Check bids before every delivery decision.
