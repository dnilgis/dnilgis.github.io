About These Reports

📊 WASDE Report

World Agricultural Supply and Demand Estimates. Monthly snapshot of global supply/demand balance for major crops and livestock. Released around the 10th of each month at 12:00 PM ET.

⚡ High impact — frequently moves corn/soybean futures 10–40¢ at release

📈 Crop Progress

Weekly condition ratings (Excellent/Good/Fair/Poor/VP) and planting/harvest progress by state. Released Mondays at 4:00 PM ET during growing season (April–November).

⚡ Moderate impact — Good/Excellent % ratings move corn and soybean prices

📦 Export Sales

Weekly net sales of corn, soybeans, and wheat to foreign buyers. Released Thursdays at 8:30 AM ET by USDA FAS. Shows how US export commitments are tracking vs USDA targets.

⚡ Moderate-high impact — large weekly sales moves futures, especially if China is buying

🌱 Prospective Plantings

Farmer survey of intended spring planting acres for corn, soybeans, wheat, and other crops. Released late March. Sets the tone for the entire marketing year.

⚡ Very high impact — major price mover if acres differ significantly from trade expectations

🏗️ Grain Stocks

Quarterly inventory of corn, soybean, wheat, sorghum, and oats on farms and in commercial facilities. Released in January, March, June, and September.

⚡ High impact — September stocks especially critical for carry-out estimates

📐 Acreage Report

Actual planted and harvested acreage for major crops based on June 1 survey. Released late June. Often the most significant report of the year for price direction.

⚡ Very high impact — frequently the biggest single-day price move of the crop year

🔭 USDA Ag Outlook Forum

Annual February forum with USDA's first official projections for the new crop year. Sets baseline supply/demand expectations before spring planting decisions are made.

⚡ Moderate impact — first look at new crop year price and acreage projections

🌾 Crop Production

Detailed crop production estimates updated monthly August through November, with the January final estimate. The August report delivers the first objective yield survey for corn and soybeans.

⚡ High impact — August report often the most market-moving of the year
Release times are approximate. USDA may shift dates around holidays. Always verify at usda.gov. 2026 dates marked "est." are estimates based on prior-year patterns. All times Eastern.
