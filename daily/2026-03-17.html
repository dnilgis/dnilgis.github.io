Why Livestock and Grains Move Together (Until They Don't)
Historically, livestock and grain futures move inversely — cheaper feed costs boost livestock margins and vice versa. But during extreme technical moves like today, both complexes can divorce from fundamentals entirely. Algorithmic trading amplifies these disconnects, creating temporary opportunities for patient producers. The key is distinguishing between technical washouts and genuine supply/demand shifts.
