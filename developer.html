Data Feeds (Live)

These JSON endpoints are publicly available and update automatically via GitHub Actions.

GET https://agsist.com/data/prices.json Commodity prices for corn, soybeans, wheat, energy, metals, and crypto. Updated every 30 min on weekdays. Includes close, net change, % change, and 52-week range.
GET https://agsist.com/data/markets.json Prediction market data (Kalshi + Polymarket) for agricultural and economic events. Updated daily. Returns active contracts with probability and volume.
GET https://agsist.com/data/daily.json Today's AI-generated daily briefing in JSON. Includes headline, summary, and price context. Generated by 6 AM CT on weekday mornings.

Usage Policy

  • Data feeds are free for non-commercial farm, extension, and co-op use.
  • Commercial licensing and white-labeling: contact us.
  • Do not hammer the endpoints — please cache data and limit to one fetch per 15 minutes or longer.
  • Attribution appreciated: link back to agsist.com where reasonable.
