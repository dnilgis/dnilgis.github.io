🌽 Ag Impact (D1+)

D1 and above triggers short-term agricultural impacts: wilting, fire risk, pasture stress, and reduced hay yields. D2+ affects crop yields — especially corn at pollination and soybeans during pod fill.

💧 When It Matters Most

Corn is most vulnerable July–August (pollination, grain fill). Soybeans are sensitive July–September. Winter wheat is at risk during fall establishment and spring green-up.

📊 Crop Insurance Note

Drought monitor status affects eligibility for some crop insurance products (WFRP, PRF, Pasture/Range/Forage). Ask your Farmers First agent if D2+ conditions persist in your county.
